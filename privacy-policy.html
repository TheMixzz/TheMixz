Privacy Policy

Introduction

TheMixz is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and security while you use our bot’s features, including games, commands, and customization tools. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we gather, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

Information Collection

We collect specific data to improve and personalize your experience with TheMixz, ensuring continuity, customization, and responsive interactions:

  • User IDs: Essential for identifying users and storing preferences across games, interactions, and relationships.
  • Messages: Necessary for processing your commands, managing game states, and providing tailored responses.
  • Server Information: Collected to enhance bot functionalities, such as custom emojis and personalized game settings.

Data Usage

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate commands, interactions, and maintain game continuity.
  • Customize features based on your preferences, from relationship tracking to game difficulty.
  • Ensure the security and smooth operation of TheMixz, providing you with a seamless experience.

Data Sharing

Your information remains confidential with us. We do not share your data with third parties except with your consent or when legally required.

Data Security

We employ strict technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption and secure data storage practices, to protect your information from unauthorized access, modification, or misuse.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Review the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data if it is inaccurate or no longer necessary.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via our support server.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy as our practices evolve. Significant updates will be communicated in our Discord server, and the latest version will always be accessible in the bot’s profile and via the help command.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this privacy policy, please join our support server. We’re here to assist you.
